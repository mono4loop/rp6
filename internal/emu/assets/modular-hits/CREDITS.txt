rp6 "modular-hits" sample kit
=============================

48 pad samples (A1..H6) for the rp6 P-6 emulator, laid out like the hardware's
8 banks (A-H) x 6 pads. Run with:

    rp6 -emu samples/modular-hits          # or: RP6_EMU_SAMPLES=samples/modular-hits rp6

(To actually hear it, build with the audio backend: `make run` / `-tags capture`.)

Source & license
----------------
All WAV files are one-shot hits from the "Modular-Hits" pack by publicsamples
(percussive/tonal hits recorded from a modular synth rig):

    https://github.com/publicsamples/Modular-Hits/tree/master/WAV

See that repo for licensing/provenance of the original recordings. Files are
used as-is (stereo, 16-bit, 44.1kHz PCM) -- no conversion needed.

Unlike the other synth-derived kits (retro-pads/low-register/mid-register/
high-register), this pack is made of distinct one-shot hits rather than
chromatic runs of the same patch, so each pad here is a genuinely different
sound (no pitch-shifted repeats).

Bank layout
-----------
A  kicks              E  bass hits
B  snares              F  chord hits (1/2)
C  hats                G  chord hits (2/2)
D  toms                H  fx / textures

Each pad within a bank is a different take from that category, picked spread
across the source folder so the 6 pads per bank cover a variety of hits
rather than near-duplicates.

Replace any file with your own <PadLabel>.wav (e.g. A1.wav) to customize a pad.
